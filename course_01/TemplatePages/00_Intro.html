Week 2: General Space Planning

Introduction

This week we will continue what you learned last semester about space planning and how we feel comfortable in our homes, conversation areas, and claiming personal space. By the end of the week you will be able to consider family personal habits of claiming personal space and have a greater appreciation as to why it is necessary to have furniture in comfortable seating areas in our home.

Reading for this Week

Designing Interiors Text Chapter 7 pages 205-211, Chapter 8 pages 217, 221-238

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this week, you should be able to:

Unit 1:

  • 1.1 Discuss space planning theory.
  • 1.2 Evaluate changes in lifestyles that affect space planning.

Unit 2:

  • 2.1 Recognize and apply basic space planning rules.
  • 2.2 Identify and create proper traffic flow.
  • 2.3 Draw floo plans for variou rooms in the home.
  • 2.4 Apply space planning techniqes for small space and large spaces.
