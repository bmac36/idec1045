Rationale

  • Outline the basic natural and synthetic fibre history, production processes and uses in fabrics today.

Overview

This is a research assignment that will put to use what you have learned this week and increase your learning about different fabrics. You will be required to recognize certain weaves, pattern, and fabric content and weight.
