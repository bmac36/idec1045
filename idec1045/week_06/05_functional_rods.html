Functional Rods

Simple steps to measure windows

Functional rods are often not visible on the treatment. They are rather inexpensive and simple in design to allow a treatment to hang or operate with ease but are not part of the overall style or offer any decorative touch to the drape. These kinds of rods rarely change over the decades as they do not follow design trends.
