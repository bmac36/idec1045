How to Succeed in an Online Course

How is this course delivered?

This course is delivered fully online.

How will I be evaluated in this course?

This course is graded by points through several areas of evaluation. You will be required to complete several assignments, and a final project to attain your grades. You must earn a final mark of at least 65%.

You can learn more about the evaluation for this course in the Evaluations module

How do I communicate with the instructor and other students?

Students are expected to communicate with other students and the instructor through the course mail. If participation in a discussion forum is required, it will be noted in the course module.

How does group work take place?

Working in groups can be a rewarding experience. When you share knowledge, resources and cases, you are able to enrich the learning of your peers. Health care teams depend on one another to enhance patient care, so many of the assignments in this program will require group work.

By demonstrating you can effectively work in a group during this course, you will ensure you are prepared to collaborate as a team when out in the community providing care. Be respectful of one another and value the contributions of each member of your group; work as a team.

How do I enroll in a group?

Once you enroll in a group, you will get access to the group discussion board and group dropboxes.

1. From the Course Tools menu on the course page, select Groups.

 

2. Click on Choose Group.

 

3. To choose a group, click on the button beside one of the group names (1). In order to see who is already enrolled in a group, click on the blue number to the left of the group (under Members) (2).

 

4. Click on Select to finish the process. You are now enrolled in a group!

What are the expectations for written assignments?

All written assignments must be written to meet post-graduate expectations in terms of quality. All written assignments to be written in 3rd person.

Essays must be properly cited using in-text citations and references following APA style. Assignments with significant writing issues that interfere with comprehension, containing plagiarism and/or missing proper documentation may be levied further penalties and deductions from those indicated on assignment rubrics.
