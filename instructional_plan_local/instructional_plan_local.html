Course Specific Notes

  • You will need to purchase a text "Designing Interiors" 2nd by Rosemary Kilmer and W. Otie Kilmer.  It is the only text you will need for all courses except for AutoCAD.  You can purchase at the Waterloo bookstore or on line.
  • Supplies needed by week 4:  Staedtler Mars Home furnishings template 1/4" scale, # 977 129, Staedtler Mars  Home planning and layout template 1/4" scale,  # 977 113, mechanical pencil nib size 0.5 or 0.7, architect ruler in imperial measure showing 1/4", white eraser, 1/4" pad of graph paper.  By week 6 have a colour wheel pocket size is fine.  For final project you will need a permanent pigment liner nib size 0.5 and a sheet of foam board in white or black cut to 15" x 20" in size and 3/16" thick, contact cement.
