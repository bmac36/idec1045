Navigating the Course

For the best user experience, we recommend using Firefox when viewing this course. If you're not sure which browser you are using, you can use this site to check, and to download another one if you need to.

Moving Through Course Content


  1. Table of Contents

    The Table of Contents on the left allows you to navigate through the modules and submodules. Click on the module or submodule title in the table of contents to open it.

  2. Navigation Arrows

    In each content page, there are navigation arrows towards the top right of the page that allow you to move from one page to the next.

  3. Breadcrumbs

    Finally, the breadcrumb trail at the top of the page can take you back to a different area in the course.
