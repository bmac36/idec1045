Layering

Layer window treatments can make the mundane extraordinary


Why Layer?

The nice thing about window treatments is that you can add or take away from them if you want a new look or tire of a treatment you have had for a long time. Layering is the way to do that. If you have a valance, you can always add a blind underneath or if you have a set of drapes with a valance you can remove the valance for a change in the room.
