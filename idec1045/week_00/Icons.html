A Note about Icon Usage

Throughout the course you will notice icons indicating actions or points of interest. These are listed here for your reference:

Icon Description

Try it yourself

Take your learning offline! You will be asked to go do something simple to apply the content from the course so you can learn through practicing.

Example icon

A story that Linda has included that will help clarify an idea.

Helpful Hint/Did you know?

A Fact or tidbit of information that will get you thinking or applying useful information in your everyday interior designing. These are the pro-tips!

Activity

Learning is about experimenting and doing. These activities are designed to help you practice the course material and learn from the choices you make.

Reflection

A simple question that we will pose. After reflecting on the question, you can click to reveal more information. This allows you to further participate in your learning, access your previous knowledge and begin applying new ideas.

Resource

Useful handouts that will benefit you outside the class. These are downloadable pdfs of some useful takeaways.

Readings

Mandatory readings from the Designing Interiors textbook.
