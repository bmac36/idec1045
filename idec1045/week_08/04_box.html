Activity: Box

Box pleat valances are among the most timeless styles that can suit every style. The treatment appears flat across the front yet has pleats in varying sizes across the face of the window. It can have bands of contrasting fabric for a decorative effect or can be scalloped or arched for a softer look and can even have the pleats in a different fabric. The wider the box pleat, the more modern it will look and the more pleats closer together appear more traditional.
