Glass Block

Scale and direction arrow symbols should be put on the right side of your drawing along with your name at all times. Scale should be shown as: Scale 1/4'” = 1’, Direction arrow should be about 2 squares by 2 squares each way and show what direction the natural light is coming into the room from. It should be judged by the largest window/door area. Placing all this information in one area on the right side of your page allows the reader to easily find important information and look more professional.

Screening

The drapery will be drawn in the room right against a window. The symbol shows the folds of drapery that come to the floor. An average single panel should be 18-24” in width. Make the folds of the drape end into the wall for neatness. Only full length sheers or drapery will be shown with this symbol. If you have blinds, shutters or valances they cannot be shown on the floor plan because they are on the wall and do not touch the floor.

Coir

The lighting symbols can be found on the Home planning and layout template with the various sizes of circles. The right bottom of the template has the electrical symbols for plugs etc.

Draw the plugs and switches right against the wall. For a switch it doesn’t matter what way you show the S at the end of the line. It can be sideways or straight.
