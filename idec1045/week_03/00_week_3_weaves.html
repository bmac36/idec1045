Week 3: Weaves, Patterns and Finishes

Introduction

Most people know little about fabrics other than how they look. This week we will look in more detail at all types of fabric to understand that there are different kinds of weaves that produce a certain look in window fabric. We will discover some fabric finishes that are applied at the manufacturing level to enhance materials and learn about the different fabric weights and how they can be used in certain window treatments. We will also go over some important terminology that is used in the window treatment industry and is important to recognize. All of this information will give you more insight into the world of retail fabric and allow you to select with more skill what kind of fabric will suit various window treatments.

Reading for this Week

Designing Interiors

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this week, you should be able to:

Unit 1:

  • 1.3 Outline fabric construction such as yarns and weaves.
  • 1.4 Outline fabric finishing techniques such as soil resistance and decorative finishes.

  • 2.4 Discuss fabric weights and their uses in home decor.
