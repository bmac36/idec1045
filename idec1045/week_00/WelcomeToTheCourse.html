Welcome to the Course

Hello and welcome to Fabric and Window Treatments!

In this course we will use methods, concepts and theories to understand and develop floor plans and read blueprints. Over the next 14 weeks, we will be drawng room dimensions and wall elevations to scale, as well as learning to identify blueprint symbols. Through hands-on assignments and practice, you will be experimenting with furniture layouts, rooms settings and creating presentation boards that will enhance your knowledge of stand industry measurements and space planning techniques. Whether you are here for your own enjoyment or as a step towards a new career, this course will set you up for success through showing you the keys to creating clean and well thought out floor plans and building creative presentations.
