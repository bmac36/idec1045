Bio

I am a graduate of the Part time Interior Decorating Program from Conestoga College and have been teaching this course for the past couple of years. I own my own Interior Decorating business and do client consultations in my home area. I am also a real estate agent in my, and surrounding areas. I spent many years in the corporate world and now love what I am doing in the decorating and real estate business that seem to go hand in hand to the benefit of all my clients. I have a real zest for life and am a great multi-tasker who loves to be busy.

My family is very important to me and we have a close relationship including my husband, 2 grown children and 2 grandchildren. I am originally from Scotland and my husband and I travel back there often. With my love of decorating, architecture and history our trips include searching out places that support my interests and luckily my husband has come to enjoy them as much as I do!
