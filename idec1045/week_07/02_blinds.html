Blinds

Hard blinds come in many styles and we will look at each individually to describe them


Ready-made or Custom?

Blinds can be purchased ready- made or custom. Like soft treatments the quality and fit will always be better with custom selections and the selection is vastly different between the two. Ready -made is often found at big box stores and can be suitable for short term use, however for a final treatment or long term use, custom treatments will be a less costly investment over time. Blinds in general are easy maintenance and can be cleaned with a soft cloth or vacuuming.


Sizing
