Week 10: Assignment

Introduction

This week is an assignment only. You have been working hard all semester learning to draw and add more detail to your drawings and presentation.

This week is to allow you to consider only 1 room but to create multiple space plan ideas for the same space.

This is important to learn to give yourself and your client options for the same space.

Use the rest of this week to work on your final project. (See Week 11)

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this week, you should be able to:

  • 2.1 Recognize and apply basic space planning rulers.
  • 2.2 Identify and create proper traffic flow.
  • 2.3 Draw floor plans for various rooms in the home.
