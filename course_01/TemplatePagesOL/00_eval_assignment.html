Assignment # 5: Space Planning Options

Due: By the last day of the week (11:55 p.m.) of Week 10
Submit to: Space Planning Options Submission Folder
Value of assignment: 5% of your final grade
Evaluation: Space Planning Options Rubric

Rationale

  • Develop floor plan layouts for various rooms of the house
  • Identify conditions and limitations involved in space planning, including standard rooms and furniture dimensions

Overview

The assignment is the same room but showing three (3) different ways to create a room layout using the space planning rules. It comprises of three (3) different room options for the same room as a drawing assignment.

This is a practice assignment to have the student consider more than one option to space plan a room. It offers the owner of the space an opportunity to move furniture at different times of the year and/or to consider using different furniture than they may be used to having in their home.

Instructions

  • Draw a floor plan that is 12 x 20’ in size with doors and windows in any position you wish.

Specifications:

  1. No fireplace in the room
  2. In pencil on graph paper ¼l;” scale including measurements around the room
  3. Photocopy to have 3 pages of the same room
  4. In pencil, show 3 different furniture arrangements for this space
  5. Two (2) room options using the same furniture but arranged differently
  6. 3rd room option using different furniture than the 2 previous rooms
  7. Add legend relating to the furniture in the room on the right side of the page
  8. Add name, scale and direction arrow on the right side of the page
  9. Brief written description of each room describing room, style, mood, client and how the furniture fits the needs of the owners

Notes

  • Scan each drawing and submit in the assignment submission folder provided
