What is OntarioLearn?

OntarioLearn is a partnership of 24 Ontario community colleges that have pooled their resources to increase your online learning options. You can search through the OntarioLearn course inventory opens in new window to find a course that you need and then you can register with any partner college offering the course.

For information regarding OntarioLearn policies and procedures visit the OntarioLearn website opens in new window.
