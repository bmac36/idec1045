Meet Your Instructor

Linda Guthro

As a passionate advocate of good design I am excited to help participants of IDEC1075 begin to discover and develop your skills in the world of decorating, and look forward to supporting you in your continued success.

Bio

Acknowledged as an authority in Interior Decorating in the post-secondary sector, I have been teaching evenings at Conestoga College since 2000. I have developed this program which has many successful graduates thriving in this industry. Testimonies from my students have been overwhelming and I am always pleased to hear from them about their fulfilling careers. I have been the recipient of 2 Associate Faculty awards and the David J. Stewart Memorial Award for excellence in teaching.

My past includes a distinguished academic career as a graduate with high honours from Sheridan College of Interior Design. My 30+ years of experience has allowed me to become expert in many areas of decorating from furniture, paint and paper, flooring, window treatments, kitchen and bath design, new home building and renovations. I owned my own successful business and store location for many years. I have hosted countless decorating seminars and have been a keynote speaker at numerous events during my career sharing my knowledge with the public.

In my spare time I love reading, architecture, camping and cooking. I have a young Havanese dog named Sabine (Beanie) who is my little sweetheart and constant companion.

As your instructor, I am excited to welcome you to the course and to remind you that we are embarking on this learning experience together. What you learn in this course will be directly applicable to your personal use or career development so I encourage you to make the most of our time together.
