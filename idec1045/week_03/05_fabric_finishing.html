Introduction to Fabric Finishing

While you may not readily recognize fabric finishing techniques when viewing or feeling a material, it is important to understand that there are many treatments that can be applied to fabric once it is produced. There is a lot that goes into making a fabric for retail use that can enhance the look, durability and even cost of a material you use in your home.

There are two basic kinds of finishing techniques applied after the production of the fiber:

  • Functional Finishing
  • Decoritive Finishing.

We will each both of these methods over the next two topics.
