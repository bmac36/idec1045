Wool Production

There are several steps to wool production from Raw materials, shearing, grading and sorting, cleaning and scouring, carding, and spinning.

Scroll down the page to reveal each step in the manufacturing process of wool.

Wool Facts

Wool can be dyed at various stages of manufacturing and can be knitted or woven into a variety of fabrics. This is a durable fabric with good elasticity. It resists soiling and provides great insulation.
