Week 9: Blueprints and History of Metric

Introduction

This week you will be learning the basics about blueprints. It is important for a decorator to have a basic understanding of a blueprint in case you are working with trades and clients on new or renovation construction. Being able to identify some of the items included in a blueprint can be a valuable tool for a decorator to use with their client. Secondly, we will cover a brief history of measurement both imperial and metric. This is a fun insight to how ancient societies measured so accurately and created such magnificent buildings before standard measure.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this week, you should be able to:

Unit 3:

  • 3.1 Identify basic blueprints and symbols.
  • 3.2 Examine purpose and uses of wall elevations.

Unit 5:

  • 5.1 Basic conversion of imperial measure to metric.
  • 5.2 Examine brief history of measurement.
