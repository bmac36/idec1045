Colour

Colour is an exciting topic because it is constantly changing. As decorators we are always learning about colour. We are surrounded by colour everywhere, every day, all over the world. From the moment we get up and decide what to wear to our food, flowers and trees, work spaces and our home, we are constantly seeing colour and reacting to it.

Selecting a colour to paint our home however can be a daunting task and can be very intimidating for the average person. Professionals have been trained to understand how colour will look in a room and can help the general public get the feeling they want from using colour. Of course in decorating we also use colour for the objects we put into our homes like furniture, flooring, window treatments and accessories.

Colour is one of our most powerful decorating tools and even a can of paint can have a huge impact in any room for little cost.
