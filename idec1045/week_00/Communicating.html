Contacting Your Instructor

If you have a question about course content or policy for the course, the preferred method for communicating with your instructor is to start a thread in the Questions for the Instructor discussion board. You should look first in the discussion board to see if another learner has already asked the same question.

If, on the other hand, you have a personal concern, you may wish to send a private email to the instructor through eConestoga's Course Mail system. To send an email to your instructor, access the course's Classlist via the drop-down menu at the top of the page.

From the Classlist window, select your instructor's name, and then choose the Course Mail link at the bottom of the list of names. In the new window that opens, write your message, attach any files if you need to, and press Send.
