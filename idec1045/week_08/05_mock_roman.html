Mock Roman Valance

A mock roman valance is meant to look like an operating roman shade but is stationary. It has folds sewn to look like a waterfall as a roman shade would look if it was raised up on the window. This is an attractive and tailored style that suits most styles of décor. As with the box pleat the hem and header are 12” in length needed. Outside mounts will have a return as well.
